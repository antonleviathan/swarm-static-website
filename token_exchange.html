Upgrade your Swarm tokens!

How and why to exchange your old swarm tokens for new ones.

Swarm 2.0 fundamentally repurposes the initial concept of Swarm 1.0 to create new market infrastructure to act as a gateway for fiat money to be invested in the promising field of crypto finance.


With the new ERC20 tokens, investors will receive access to opportunities of owning cryptos backed by real-world assets (renewable energy, crypto hedge fund, Saas, rainforest preservation, blockchain enterprises, etc...) and avoid volatility significantly.

The process is very simple and we provide clear instructions.


1. Login to the wallet where you have your Swarm tokens

2. Select your swarm tokens and click send



3. Enter burn address: 1CounterpartyXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXUWLpVr

4. Enter the total number of swarm tokens you own.

5. Select a Bitcoin Fee (any priority will do).

6. Click "Send".



7. Copy the address from which you sent your swarm tokens.



8. Go to https://xchain.io and paste your address in the top right corner and click search.



9. Go to the tab “Sends” and click on “view” on your Swarm’s transaction (you might have to wait a while until your transaction shows in this part).



10. Copy your Transaction Hash and send it to burn1.0@swarm.fund with the Ethereum address you’d like us to send your new Swarm tokens to.



And that’s it! We appreciate your time and effort.
